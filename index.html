Bank Loan Default Likelihood

Classifcation/Regression model in Python

Predicted default likelihood and severity with .653 MAE

In Python, I used PCA and additional feature construction techniques to clean a large dataset of customer data. I implemented a stochastic gradient descent classifier to binary classify likelihood of a customer defaulting on a loan, and when a default was predicted I used a gradient forest regressor to estimate the severity of the default.
